Hearth.io was the project that didn't ship. The repo is still on GitHub.

Hearth was an attempt to bring engineering thinking to non-profit work: a
tool for mission-driven organizations to do the kind of systems work that
technology companies take for granted. It didn't ship. The question still
feels worth asking: how do you put working software in service of people
whose mission isn't software?
